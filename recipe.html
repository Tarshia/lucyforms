This is a Githeri Recipe for two:-

Let us learn to cook a delicious githeri:

Githeri is a Kenyan cuisine primarily being a mixture of Maize grains and Bean grains.

The ingredients are:

  • Maize grains
  • Bean grains
  • Cooking Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • 5 litres of pure water.

The Procedure is;

  1. Boil 20 grams of clean Yellow bean grains for 32 minutes in 2 litres of pure water;
  2. Add 20 grams of clean white maize grains to the boiled beans;
  3. Reboil the mixture for 30 minutes in 3 litres of pure water;
  4. Remove the boiled mixture from fire;
  5. Fry a handful of chooped onions (bulb or leafy) in 3 spoonfuls of cholestrol-free cooking oil -
    care is taken not to char the frying onions - note the color change, from pink/green to brown -
    for about 3 minutes under a non-luminous heat;
  6. Add a handful of chopped ripe tomatoes to the seam-fried onions and 'stir' the mixture and leave it to fry for 2.5 minutes;
  7. Add a quarter spoonful of table salt to the frying groceries and stir;
  8. After half a minute, pour the boiled mixture of Maize and Bean grains to the salted fried groceries;
  9. Stir the contents and seam them for 5 minutes and remove from fire.

Our delicious githeri is ready to serve two. Serve while warm. Githeri can be taken with ripe avocado, tea, or gruel/porridge.
